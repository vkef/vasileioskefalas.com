My name is Vasilis and I am a junior Web Developer and Programmer, currently 25 years old based in Athens, Greece. I am taking lessons in Informatics and Telecommunications at University of Peloponnese and my primary focus and insiration is always coding something new. I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques. I am both driven, self-motivated and very passionate about Web Development, and strive to better myself as a developer.

I am familiar in both front and back-end development, and I am competent in HTML/HTML5, CSS/CSS3, Tailwind, SASS, Bootstap, JavaScript, PHP, Laravel and SQL/MySQL. I am also experienced in non-web-based languages, including C, Python, Tex, Matlab and Assemblyx84. I have also worked on some smaller C projects, and have used the language to create one-time use tools and fun little console games.

Custom created sites are my passion and I seek to create ones that can easily be optimized for aesthetic appeal on each of desktops, tablets and cell phones. This site is fully customized, the graphics and design are created by @candiasouranaki. The main idea is based on simplicity, functionality and minimalism. Furthermore it has fully responsive intergration for every resolution.
